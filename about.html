About Saucony Creek Deli

The newly opened Saucony Creek Deli provides Kutztown with the authentic deli experience that it has been missing. Come in early for a wide variety of breakfast foods and later on for Hoagies, Burgers and more. All with the option to eat in , carry out, or get free delivery. All food is made fast and fresh by the head chef Doug Grym.
